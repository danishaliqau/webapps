Radiology

03 Feb 2019
NORI Hospital

Radiology Department of NORI is equipped with state of the art equipment. The department has internationally experienced doctors and skilled technologists to provide the best quality imaging, diagnostic, and treatment services in an efficient environment ensuring maximum care, patient safety. The department is fully integrated with the Oncology, Nuclear Medicine and Pathology departments at AECH- NORI.

Our specialized team performs and interpret countless examinations each year. All our machines are integrated with the HMIS system and the images are available to the clinicians through PACS. The number of cases, integrated with ongoing research and education, results in wide-ranging expertise in imaging and treatment.

⦁ Radiology department is at the forefront of breast cancer screening program
⦁ NORI offered stereotactic biopsy, the latest biopsy of its kind for the first time in public hospital in Rawalpindi/ Islamabad
⦁ Multiple interventional procedures like ultrasound guided and CT guided biopsies are being routinely carried out at discounted prices.

Services

X Rays

All kinds of X-rays are being performed by qualified technologists. Procedures are performed on radiation safety..

Mammaography

Radiology department is at the forefront of breast cancer screening program. We have state of the art 3D mammography..

Ultrasound

Our services include ultrasound of the abdomen and pelvis, chest, KUB, breast and axilla, subcutaneous tissues..

CT Scan

At NORI, we perform multiple with and without contrast CT studies for chest, abdomen, pelvis, and head and neck, including CT angiographies, with reports ready within 1-2 days and all data saved in our HMIS and PACS systems.

MRI

We have a 1.5 Tesla GE MR machine with dedicated coils for comprehensive imaging of the brain, head and neck, spine, abdomen, and limbs.

Interventional Procedures

Advanced interventional procedures at NORI ensure precise and minimally invasive diagnostic and therapeutic options.
