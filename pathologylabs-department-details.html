Pathology Labs

03 Feb 2019
NORI Hospital

AECH NORI pathology laboratories provide wide range of quality diagnostic services which help in accurate diagnosis, treatment and prevention of diseases. The dedicated team at AECH NORI Pathology Labs consists of highly skilled professionals committed to deliver cost-effective diagnostic services with a strong emphasis on quality in various fields such as Hematology, Clinical chemistry and Immunoassay, Molecular Diagnostics, Microbiology, and Histopathology. Pathology Labs of AECH NORI are well equipped with state of the art technology. NORI has achieved the prestigious ISO-15189 accreditation from Pakistan National Accreditation Council (PNAC). We also hold ISO 9001:2015 certification which reinforces our commitment to continuous improvement in all aspects of our operations. Our department is also recognized by CPSP for FCPS training in Hematology and Histopathology. We are very proud of our work. The Diagnostic Department strives to maintain a balanced approach, prioritizing service excellence, education, and quality. We not only serve the patients from our own Hospital but also referred from other hospitals and clinicians. Our main concern is on time reporting with minimum turnaround time, as any delay in reporting can put patients' lives at risk. Notably, the department has invested substantial effort in improving the quality services. .
