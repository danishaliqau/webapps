The Department of Clinical and Radiation Oncology is equipped with the most advanced technology to deliver both external beam radiothertherapy and brachytherapy. The equipment available for external-beam therapy include three linear accelerators (VARIAN UNIQUE, TRUEBEAM, VARIAN DUAL ENERGY CLINAC IX), one Cyberknife (ACCURAY), one Cobalt-60 with MLSs (EQUINOX Co-60), one Brachytherapy Co-60 HDR with interstitial needles, 4-D CT Simulator and MR Simulator with respiratory gating We are providing the facility of Stereotactic Radiosurgery (SRS), Stereotactic Body Radiotherapy (SBRT), Intensity-Modulated Radiation Therapy (IMRT), Volumetric Modulated Arc Therapy (VMAT), LATTICE Radiotherapy, Electron Therapy and high-dose rate (HDR) brachytherapy in gynaecological cancers including intracavitary and interstitial brachytherapy.

Intensity Modulated radiotherapy (IMRT) and Volumetric Modulated Arc Therapy (VMAT)
Intensity Modulated radiotherapy (IMRT) and Volumetric Modulated Arc Therapy (VMAT) are the most advanced and precise radiotherapy techniques. To deliver IMRT/VMAT the institute is equipped with the most advanced Varian Single Energy Linear Accelerator and True Beam Linear Accelerator. Using these Techniques we can deliver highest doses of radiation to the tumor and spare surrounding normal structures and hence greatly reduce the side effects of radiotherapy
