NORI Hospital

The department of Radiation and Clinical Oncology is providing state of the art treatment facilities to the cancer Patients since 1986. With the advancements in technology and expertise the department has evolved over the past years and is well equipped with the most advanced Radiotherapy Machines, experienced radiation & clinical oncologists, nurses and technical staff. We provide the latest treatment facilities meeting the national and international standards.

Distinction: First female FCPS in Radiation Oncology in Pakistan

Position: Consultant Clinical and Radiation Oncologist
Member Faculty of Radiation Oncology, College Of Physicians & Surgeons Pakistan
Senior Scientific Member of PSCO
Research Committee Member of FARO

Qualification: MBBS (PAK, 1992), FCPS in Clinical Oncology (PAK, 1998), FRCP (GLAS, UK, 2018)

Work Experience: More than 28 years in Oncology
Joined AECH NORI in 1996

Special Interest/Expertise:
Tumour sites of interest: Breast, CNS, Head & Neck, Gynecology, lymphomas, GI, skin, orbit.
Image guided brachytherapy (IGBT) in gynaecological cancers
Incharge Cancer awareness and screening program at NORI
Clinical trials in related tumour sites
Education and training in Oncology
Supervisor and examiner of College of Physicians & Surgeons Pakistan for more than 23 years
Member Board of Studies for Oncology of PIEAS University Islamabad Pakistan for more than 23 years
